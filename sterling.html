Sterling Engine

A Sterling engine is a type of heat engine that genreates work using temperature difference between its two plates. This was done for a class where the goal was to improve performance from the baseline design. Once the initial prototype was benchmarked, I made several design changes, the most significant of which was CNC'd grooves on the top plate to increase convection surface area. It was able to achieve a 50% higher steady state RPM compared to baseline.
